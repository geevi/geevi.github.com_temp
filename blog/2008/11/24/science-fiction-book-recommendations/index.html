Science Fiction Book Recommendations

I read a lot of science fiction. I always keep a collection of science fiction ebooks in my mobile, so that i can read even when i travel. My favorite books are
  • Nightfall by Issac Asimov: A thriller
  • Mostly Harmless by Douglas Adams : This one is very funny. Its part of a series called Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy
  • Enders Game by Orson Scott Card: An inspiring novel. This one is also the start of a series of novels.
  • Stranger in a Strange Land by Robert A. Heinlein : Can be a bit boring, but amazing on the whole.
